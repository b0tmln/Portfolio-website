Computer Vision for Expression and Mask Recognition

The purpose of this project was to develop a system that combined Convolutional Neural Networks (using DenseNet, EfficientNet and ResNet) with bounding box technology (using MTCNN) to recognize facial expressions and detect whether a person is wearing a mask. This project addressed the growing need for advanced computer vision systems in various fields, especially in the context of public health, security, and human-computer interaction.

Existing research indicates that the use of MTCNN for face detection and alignment followed by a convolutional neural network for processing is an effective method for face recognition tasks. Our project aimed to contribute to facial processing technology by applying this method to a face expression and mask recognition task.

In the end, I had:
• Developed a robust model to determine if a given face expression or whether they are wearing a mask using datasets with over 50,000 images.
• Incorporated techniques such as hyperparameter tuning and ensembling on various models.
• Resulted in a final model that had greater performance than the majority of literature analysed. Expression recognition attained an accuracy of 88.13% and 84.7% on FER-2013 whilst Mask recognition obtained 100% accuracy on FMD-Dataset

Machine Learning on the relation between blood proteins and UPDRS Tests with Parkinson's Disease

Investigated the capacity for blood-work signals in patients to indicate the MDS-UPDRS level for that patient. The blood work signals include concentrations of proteins and peptides in the patients’ blood and these signals are logged with the patients MDS-UPDRS levels at 6 month intervals.

Utilised machine learning techniques to identify if a relationship between the progression of Parkinson's disease of a patient and that patients’ blood work exists, and if it does exist, produce an accurate model of the relationship.

• Applied 5 machine learning models to predict UPDRS score relationships with protein content.
• Developed 4 models from scratch in python (Adaboost, Gradient Boost, Bagging, Ridge Regression).
• Evaluated model performance to identify the most effective predictor using plotting and MSE.
